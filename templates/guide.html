02 · The audit

Research what's real. Rate every finding.

The fix isn't a rewrite of any single asset — it's a foundation every asset can run from. Build it in three moves:

01
Interview the people who already know

Founders, sales, and three recent buyers. Where do their words diverge? That gap is the work.

02
Rate every claim with a source

Each statement gets a source and a confidence level. No black box — you can see why every line is there.

03
Write it down once

One foundation. The site, the deck, and the tooling all realign to it. Yours to keep, with or without me.
